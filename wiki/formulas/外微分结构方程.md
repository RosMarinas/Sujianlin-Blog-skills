## 标架与度规

$$
d\boldsymbol{r} = \hat{\boldsymbol{e}}_\mu \omega^\mu,\quad ds^2 = \eta_{\mu\nu} \omega^\mu\omega^\nu,\quad \langle \hat{\boldsymbol{e}}_\mu, \hat{\boldsymbol{e}}_\nu\rangle = \eta_{\mu\nu}
$$

## 第一结构方程（无挠条件）

$$
d\omega^\mu + \omega_\nu^\mu \land \omega^\nu = 0
$$

## 第二结构方程（曲率定义）

$$
\mathscr{R}_\nu^\mu = d\omega_\nu^\mu + \omega_\alpha^\mu \land \omega_\nu^\alpha
$$

## 曲率张量分量

$$
\mathscr{R}_\nu^\mu = \sum_{\beta<\gamma} \hat{R}^\mu_{\nu\beta\gamma}\omega^\beta\land\omega^\gamma = \frac12 R^\mu_{\nu\beta\gamma}\omega^\beta\land\omega^\gamma
$$

## 坐标变换

$$
R^\mu_{\nu\beta\gamma} = (h^{-1})_{\mu'}^\mu \hat{R}^{\mu'}_{\nu'\beta'\gamma'} h_\nu^{\nu'} h_\beta^{\beta'} h_\gamma^{\gamma'}
$$
