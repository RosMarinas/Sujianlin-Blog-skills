## 概述

$$p(\boldsymbol{x})=\frac{1}{\sqrt{(2\pi)^n \det(\boldsymbol{\Sigma})}}\exp\left\{-\frac{1}{2}(\boldsymbol{x}-\boldsymbol{\mu})^{\top}\boldsymbol{\Sigma}^{-1}(\boldsymbol{x}-\boldsymbol{\mu})\right\}$$
